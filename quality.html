Quality Policy

For any organization, Quality Control is the most important dimension; wherein it helps in analyzing the quality worthiness of the product/offerings which the organization has promised to its customer. To achieve the desired predetermined results, Adinath Enterprises has set up Quality Control Department.

The department has laid down standard set of policies and procedures for every phase of production. This helps the organization to ensure, the final product reaches the customer as per his requirement. The department has broadly categorized its policies and procedures with respect to:

  • Continuous monitoring and inspection of sourced raw materials as per the set parameters
  • Examining the production process from first stage to final stage
  • Providing test report along with the delivery of the products

On a whole, we at Adinath Enterprises work within the guided framework of standard policies and procedures to ensure smooth flow of quality operations.
